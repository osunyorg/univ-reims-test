However, the mechanisms by which the bacterium activates the host's immune response underlying ISR remain to be elucidated. In this study, we examined whether the extent of priming state could contribute to the PTA-271-induced ISR in Arabidopsis, by focusing on the camalexin response and CYP71A12 gene expression after B. cinerea and Pst DC3000 infection. Our data provide evidence that PTA-271 triggers ISR against both pathogens through priming camalexin synthesis in SA-and NPR1-dependent manner.
